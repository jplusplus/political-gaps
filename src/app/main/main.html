Political Gaps

How representative (or not) are our parliaments? PoliticalGaps is the first investigation on jQuest, a platform for journalism schools that lets journalism students in Europe learn datajournalism, connect with one another and investigate collaboratively.

We are investigating the representativity of elected officials in assemblies all over Europe. The data you see here is live. The database will grow more complete as the academic year continues.
